We are excited to share our recent publication, "Making atomistic materials calculations accessible with the AiiDAlab Quantum ESPRESSO app," which was published in npj Computational Materials. In this paper, we introduce the AiiDAlab Quantum ESPRESSO (QE) app, a web-based platform designed to streamline the setup, execution, and analysis of Quantum ESPRESSO simulations. The app features an intuitive interface, automated protocols, and a modular plugin architecture, making advanced materials simulations more accessible, reproducible, and extensible across first-principles codes. You can read the full paper [here](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41524-025-01936-4).
