The PREMISE project has officially come to an end 🍾

We are proud of the work we have accomplished and the impact we have made in the field of ORD interoperability. We would like to thank all of our collaborators, partners, and supporters for their contributions to the project. While the project has come to an end, the work we have done will continue to have an impact in the field. We will continue to maintain and support the tools and resources we have developed, and we look forward to seeing how they will be used and built upon in the future.
